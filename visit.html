Getting here

How to arrive.

Underground

Baker Street (Bakerloo, Circle, Hammersmith & City, Jubilee, Metropolitan) — 4 minutes walk
Bond Street (Central, Jubilee, Elizabeth) — 8 minutes walk
Marylebone (Bakerloo) — 10 minutes walk

By bus

Routes 2, 13, 30, 74, 113, 139, 189, 274 all stop on Baker Street or Marylebone Road.

Cycling

Santander Cycles docking stations on Marylebone High Street and Dorset Square.

By car

Q-Park Marylebone (Cramer Street) is the closest car park. The area is within London's Congestion Charge zone.

Good to know

Before you come.

Do you accept walk-ins?+

Yes — the bar counter is kept for walk-ins and we're happy to fit you in where we can. For tables, we recommend booking through OpenTable.

Is there a dress code?+

No formal dress code. Come as you are — we've designed the room to feel comfortable in jeans or a suit.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements?+

Absolutely. Please let us know about any allergies or dietary needs when you book, and we'll make sure the kitchen is ready for you. Vegetarian options are available throughout the menu.

Are children welcome?+

Yes — children are welcome at all services. Just let us know when booking so we can prepare the right table.

Is the restaurant accessible?+

The ground floor dining room is fully accessible, with an accessible WC. Please let us know in advance if you have any specific requirements — we'll do everything we can to accommodate.

Is there a service charge?+

A discretionary service charge of 12.5% is added to all bills.

Do you have a private dining room?+

Yes — we host private events for groups of 10 or more. Learn more about private dining at Clio →

Do you have gift vouchers?+

Yes, in any denomination. Email hello@cliorestaurant.co.uk and we'll arrange one for you.
